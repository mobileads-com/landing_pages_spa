frequently asked questions

What happens if I have exceeded the ad serving credit limit for the month?

You will be notified, through email, on the exceeded amount of ad serving impressions and the chargeable exceeded amount. The exceeded amount will be included in your monthly post-paid bill.

Why are you giving us free lifetime account?

Paying for creatives just for pitching ideas to brands and advertisers can dig into your pockets. Which methods of payment do you accept?

We take our payments through credit card or Paypal. So, if you have a Paypal account, you can just use that. If you don't have a Paypal account, you can quickly and easily pay using Visa and Mastercard.

If your organization does not allow Paypal or credit card payments, please contact us at contact@mobileads.com to arrange an alternative payment method.

We can issue you with an invoice and accept payment via Bank Transfer. This option is only available for long-term subscriptions. Monthly subscriptions have to be processed via Paypal or credit card.

What if I have paid and decided to upgrade to a higher plan?

No problem. Simply change your plan through your account settings, choose a new plan and agree to the new price plan. Your new pricing plan will not deduct payment from your account until the next month's billing cycle.

When do I pay? And do I pay monthly, quarterly or annually?

When you want to run an active campaign, you will have to subscribe to a plan. At the beginning of every billing cycle, you will receive an email asking you to pay to continue using MobileAds. At this time, you can cancel with no obligation or hassle.

Your credit card or Paypal account will be charged automatically every month. If you prefer to pay for a specific amount of months or annually, you can contact us at contact@mobileads.com, and we will be happy to help. There are no cancellation or other hidden fees!

What are the free account limitations?

You will not be able to generate ad tags or run active campaigns using the free version.

Using MobileAds for free, you can create and manage ads, change the platform logo to your own brand, mask your preview links to your own preferred URL, and share the preview links with your clients for demo or pitching, The full white label solution is only available for Pro and Enterprise plan.
